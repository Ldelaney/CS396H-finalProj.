About SyllabusScheduler

What it does!

Reads your syllabi and makes a calendar of classes and deadlines/due dates.
Connects with your google calendar so you can have all of your events in one place.

Who needs it?

Students who are tired of retyping their syllabi in order to add the relevant details to their calendars.
Students who forget things.
